Tension controls:

  • a: Counter-rotate slowly
  • s: Counter-rotate slightly quicker
  • d: Increase tension strength
  • f: Increase tension strength
You can hold multiple buttons at once to compound their effects. Most of the time, just holding one of a or s will decrease tension a little but not actively counter-rotate.
Holding all four at once quickly puts tension at neutral.
Float picking: Hold "s" to counter-rotate slightly, then tap "a" carefully to control the tension position.
