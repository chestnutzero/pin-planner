Instructions:
To move pins, click a pin to select it, then click where you want it to go
Add chambers with the button - they'll be populated with a randomly sized key and driver pin.
Resize pins using the + and - buttons after selecting a pin.
Once selected, you can edit pins and move their points around or create new points by clicking anywhere on the pin
You can export a selected pin and copy the pin definition from the text box below. Then share it or add it into your other pinning visualizations by importing
Option/alt-click a pin, chamber, or point on pin to delete it.

All changes are saved automatically in the URL, which is long-lived (no promises though) and shareable.
